# EXO ONE — observation report & incorporation map
(researched 2026-07-04; sources: Wikipedia, Grokipedia, KeenGamer, GameRamble,
TheSixthAxis, TheGamer, Steam community)

## 1. How EXO ONE constructs its "messages"
- CHANNEL: muffled/distorted human voice + readable subtitles with speaker
  tags ("Voice", Clara). The distortion is DIEGETIC: you are the machine -
  you cannot parse human language as words, so it arrives as interference.
- TEXTURE: short abstract fragments, "lore snippets", no explicit guidance;
  static-laced; sparse.
- CADENCE: drips at traversal moments (triggered, not menu'd); a signal you
  FOLLOW planet to planet supplies direction; story rides in the gaps.
- FUNCTION: signal-as-instruction (the whole premise: Earth receives an
  alien signal containing INSTRUCTIONS to build the craft) - machine-grade
  messaging as narrative spine. Identical in function to our operator.

## 2. Our transmission grammar (adaptation)
- lowercase fragments joined by ellipses; 5-12 words; speaker tag kept
  ('operator:' = our 'Voice'); calm, zero melodrama; teletype = static feel.
- help/keys stay crisp (UI, not transmission).

## 3. Additional elements worth incorporating (feasibility-ranked)
A. SIGNAL BEACON (light column at each scene's point of interest) - EXO's
   core wayfinding. CHEAP: one emissive pillar prop + slow pulse. Phase-2 #1.
B. TRANSMISSION DRIPS mid-traversal - we already own the msg.at scheduler;
   write 3-5 interference fragments per scene. CHEAP. Phase-2 #2.
C. MONOLITH-SCALE silhouettes on the void/city horizon - sense of scale.
   CHEAP (2-3 giant slabs, fog does the rest). Phase-2 #3.
D. MOMENTUM/FLOW traversal (roll->glide loop, gravity dial) - their whole
   gameplay. HEAVY; our bike is the cousin. Phase-3 candidate only.
E. Continuous hypnotic score - ALREADY ALIGNED (Stellardrone universal bed).
F. Minimal HUD / no objective markers - ALREADY ALIGNED.
G. Planet-hop structure w/ title cards - ALREADY ALIGNED (scene line).
H. Distorted VO - EXCLUDED by our copy-only voice policy (text carries it).
